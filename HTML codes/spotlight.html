Shreya Goshal

Shreya Ghoshal is a renowned Indian playback singer known for her melodious and soulful voice. She has won several prestigious awards for her work in the Indian film industry, including four National Film Awards and six Filmfare Awards. With over two decades of experience in the industry, she has recorded songs in various languages such as Hindi, Bengali, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, and more. Shreya's versatility and ability to bring emotions alive through her voice have made her a popular and beloved singer among music enthusiasts worldwide.
