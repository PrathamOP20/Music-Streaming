Discover and rate the hottest beats on BeatBank - where every beat counts

Beatbank is a revolutionary music rating website created by the dynamic duo of Dr. Jhatka and Ghasitaram. Dr. Jhatka is a renowned scientist and technology expert with a passion for music, while Ghasitaram is a seasoned musician and producer with a keen ear for talent. Together, they have combined their knowledge and expertise to create a platform that not only allows users to rate and review their favorite songs, but also provides a wealth of information and resources for music enthusiasts. In addition to their work on Beatbank, Dr. Jhatka and Ghasitaram have also collaborated on a number of other creative projects, including producing music for film and television and developing cutting-edge technologies to enhance the music listening experience. Their shared love of music and dedication to innovation has made them a force to be reckoned with in the music industry, and their contributions to Beatbank are a testament to their passion and creativity.
